Screwdriver (Hosted CI)

Screwdriver enables CI/CD as a central hosted solution Jive site

We are analyzing opted-in builds for failure reasons using Cinch and forwarding this data into Splunk for a broader analysis of the health of the company’s builds. This data and analysis are critical to our ability to identify areas to address in order to provide a performant and reliable CD service for Yahoo.

Screwdriver is Yahoo’s centralized Continuous Delivery service, enabling developers to deliver their software in a fast and automated way.

About

The Cloud Services organization provides cutting edge self-serve, dynamic and elastic services enabling Yahoo developers to write, commit, and deploy code quickly and efficiently.

As part of the Yahoo Platforms & Personalization Products organization, we provide Platform as a Service (PaaS), Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS), Monitoring as a Service (MaaS), and Continuous Delivery (CD) solutions that enable and accelerate Yahoo strategic initiatives.
